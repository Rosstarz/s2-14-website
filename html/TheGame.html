THE GAME

Since its first creation in 1997, the Caesar and Cleopatra game has had several editions added to it from various different languages. The Game is based around two of the greatest leaders known in history. A quarrel between Caesar and Cleopatra turned into a captivating card game. Live vicariously through one of these rulers and try to win by either invading Egypt with Caesar’s great army, or freeing Egypt from Caesar’s shackles and seeking independence as Cleopatra.

RULES

Here are the rules to the game: In this game, you must obtain the most amount of patrician cards. There are two options. You can either choose:

1. To play actively and you must follow all of these rules

- play one action card
- play one or two influence cards
- draw cards to refill your hand
- draw the next vote of confidence card.

or

2. The second option is to play passively: You may discard cards from your hand and draw to replace them. The player with the most amount of points/patrician cards wins the game.


Components of the game:

- 50 Roman Cards

- 50 Egyptian Cards

- 6 Influence Bonus Cards

- 21 Patrician Cards (5 Groups)

- 8 Vote of Confidence Cards
